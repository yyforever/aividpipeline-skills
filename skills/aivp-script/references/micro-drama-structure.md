## Dialogue Compression

Every line must do at least one of:
1. Advance plot
2. Reveal character
3. Create tension

Ideally, every line does 2 of 3. Lines that do none get cut.

**Bad:** "Hi, how was your day?"
**Good:** "You changed the locks." (advances plot + reveals relationship state + creates tension)
